Reflect is committed to maintaining the highest security standards across its infrastructure. All audit reports are publicly available in our [audits repository](https://github.com/palindrome-eng/audits).

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="02/2026 - Offside Labs">
    This audit covers the **Reflect Core Yield Routing** infrastructure — the system responsible for directing and managing yield flows across the protocol. Key focus areas include:

    - Routing logic correctness and edge-case handling
    - Access control and permissioning for yield operations
    - Safety of cross-program invocations and account validation
    - Protection against fund misrouting or loss scenarios
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="09/2025 - Offside Labs">
    This audit covers the **Delta-Neutral Strategy** powering the USDC+ yield product. Key focus areas include:

    - Program validation and instruction integrity
    - Token minting and redemption logic
    - Access controls and privilege escalation vectors
    - Soundness of the delta-neutral position management
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
